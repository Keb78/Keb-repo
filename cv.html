Quality engeneer & technical liaison KDQ Solutions AB

October 2014 - 2021 Stockholm, Sweden

My main tasks as quality engineers were to help create solutions as part of an engineering team. I worked to find defects, determine the causes of those defects, and provide solutions to defected problems. addition to the tasks described above, I also had other duties and responsibilities, some of which include:
  • Collecting, analyzing, and summarizing data.
  • Managing external quality issues by corrective actions.
  • Implementing methods for inspection of incoming material.
  • Making sure processes adhere to quality standards.
  • Coordinating, reviewing, and submitting First Articles, PPAPs, and sample parts for customer approval
  • Communicating quality issues with customer and supplier.
  • Determining quality improvement factors.
  • Developing control and inspection plans for molding and secondary operations as well as incoming materials.
